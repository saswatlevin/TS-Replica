11) Some important formulae are given below w.r.t the "cart_item_price", "discount_amount", "discount_percentage" and "discounted_total" fields in the CartItems sub-documents. It also references the "total_price", "total_discount_amount", "total_discounted_total" and "total_discount_percentage"

"discount_amount" = "discount_percentage" * "cart_item_price"

"discounted_total" = "cart_item_price" - "discount_amount"

"total_item_total" = sum of all CartItems "item_total"(s) of a User.
            = 30 + 50 = 80

"total_discount_amount" = sum of all CartItem "discount_amount"(s) of a User.
                      = 3 + 7.5 = 10.5

"total_discounted_total" = sum of all CartItem "discounted_total"(s) of a User. 
                           = 27 + 42.5 = 69.5

"total_discount_percentage" = ("total_discount_amount" / "total_item_total") × 100
                          = (10.5 / 80) × 100
                          = 13.125%
"total_payable_amount" = ("total_item_total" - "total_discount_amount")
